Countdowns

An archive of the TV-show countdown sites I built between 2012 and 2025 — each one preserved as it shipped, with its original art, type and sound. The clocks were long expired, so I’ve re-pointed every timer to a future date: they tick live again. All of it hosted here on pokusin.com.
